This document lists all the databases that are cross referenced in HTS CSRS biocomputes. The data bases that are specifically mentioned in the "web resource" comment topic of HTS-CSRS are not listed. 

It lists databases in XR lines which are used as resources for information related to biocompute entries and are found in resources other than HTS-CSRS.

The DbXR_URL field allows to access an entry in the cross referenced database by (we will have to write our own script that allows an identifier found in a cross-reference line by the HTS-CSRS Biocompute object number of the entry.
